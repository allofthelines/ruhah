What is an Ask?

A form with which you request an outfit idea from other users.

Who creates the outfits for the Asks?

Other Ruhah users. That could be regular users or professional stylists. You have the option of only making it visible for accounts you follow. You can also create a private ask, so to only one specific account.

How are the outfits selected?

For each ask, multiple different users create and submit their own outfit idea for your ask. Then our algorithm selects one of them, which is the one you see.

What should i write in the Ask stylist notes?

Anything that could help a human get it. You can describe a mood you're into, a trend you wanna explore, a specific occasion or an idea you already have but need help with.

Can i buy the items inside the outfit?

Yes, all the items in an outfit are available for purchase.

Do i have to buy something in it?

No. You can just use the outfit as an inspiration.

Is creating and submitting an Ask free?

Yes.

Can i create an outfit for another user's Ask?

Yes, in the studio. Visit the Studio help section for more info on how it works.

What is a Ruhah Box?

A box delivered to you that includes 6-8 items. These items make for 2 different outfits, 3-4 items each, curated by human stylists based on your personal requests.

Do i have to buy everything in it?

No. You buy and keep only the items you want to. The rest you put them back to the box and return them to us for free.

How are the outfits curated?

Currently our outfit selection system includes a combination of stylist curation, public voting and decision making by our in-house fashion experts and trend analysts. In the future we will offer the option to choose a specific stylist of your choice to make your outfit.

What do i pay for?

  • A $15 styling fee to order the Box. This includes all Box shipping costs, both delivery and return.
  • The price of each item that you want to keep.
The styling fee is returned to you as discount on the items you purchased.
If you decide to keep all the items that belong to the same outfit you get -20% discount on them.

Is it a subscription service?

No. You only pay each time you order a Box.

What is the Ruhah Studio?

It is a place where users can view other users' Asks and create and submit outfits for them. It provides access to our entire catalogue of clothes and accessories which you can use for the outfits, including items from our own collection and the brands we collaborate with. In the Studio, you can browse, search, and select items to create and submit outfits quickly and easily.

How do I search for items to include in my outfit?

You can search for items using keywords that you think describe the item you have in mind and its attributes ("blue" "distressed" "stripes" "loafer" etc). We provide a keyword guide to assist you, but feel free to try different keywords if you don't find what you're looking for with the first try.

Do I need to worry about the sizes of the items I select?

No. Our item search algorithm automatically filters items to fit the size of the user that created the Ask. You, the creator, can focus purely on aesthetics and style.

What kind of items are available in the catalogue?

The catalogue includes a wide range of clothing items such as shirts, trousers, dresses, and accessories like bags and glasses. These items can be either new or pre-owned and come from our own collection or from suppliers we collaborate with. If an item appears in your search, it is available and ready to ship.

Why are some requests yellow?

The user that submitted a yellow request follows you at Ruhah.

Why are some requests red?

This is a private Ask. The user that submitted the red request specifically created this Ask for you and only you to see. In your profile settings you can choose whether you can have private Asks open, and whether they are free or you want to charge a fee.

Who is a Stylist?

At Ruhah we call stylist a registered user who creates an outfit as a response to another user's Ask request. Some users are professional stylists, others not. As a Stylist, you can sign up and start styling right away in the Studio.

How do Stylists earn money?

Every time a user buys an item from an outfit a stylist created. The amount earned is a percentage of the final price. That percentage changes depending on the occasion but the range is from 15% to 30%.

What happens after a stylist creates and submits an outfit

For each user request, up to 10 stylists recommend an outfit each. In the next few hours our algorithm selects one of them, depending on factors like the ask creator's profile and the outfit's popularity. This outfit is displayed in the creator's profile and they are free to either buy or not items from that outfit.

What happens if they don't buy anything?

Nothing.

Is there any other way to earn money?

Yes. Every time you create an outfit for an ask it appears in your profile grid. Users can double click and like your grid outfits. When they do that the clothes of that outfit are saved in their likes section. In their likes section they can generate fits and buy the clothes from these fits. You get 10% for every timem they buy that was liked from your profile grid.

Is it really that simple?

Yes. You sign up, start styling, and earn money each time your items are bought.

We currently only deliver to the UK.

Most of the clothes and accessories are delivered directly from the brands we collaborate with. After each order the brand will email to you information regarding the tracking and expected shipping time.

Disclaimer: For packages not shipped by our warehouse, we are not responsible for any lost or damaged items.
We recommend contacting the brand or carrier directly for any delivery-related issues. However, feel free to contact us for any unresolved issue or question and we will do our best to find a solution.

Items not purchased

We offer free returns on all items in your Ruhah Box that you did not purchase.
  • You have 3 days to decide what to keep and what to return.
  • Place the items you don’t want back to the Box. Make sure they are in the same condition as found with all original packaging and tags.
  • Seal the Box with the tape provided and attach the pre-paid return label outside.
  • Go to the nearest post-office and return the Box.

Items purchased

We accept returns on items purchased within 30 days. The customer is responsible for the carrier choice and the shipping cost.
  • Include a piece of paper with the original order number inside the package.
  • Attach a paper with the return address on the outside of the package.
  • Make sure to select a delivery option with a tracking number.
  • As soon as we receive the package we will refund the amount back to your payment method.
Keep in mind: It may take up to 14 business days to receive a refund. If your refund hasn’t been issued within that time, please contact us and we'll sort things out.
